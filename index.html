Upcoming Products

SIS Cloud Management System

4 modules Cloud-native ERP-ready integration Mobile & web access

The SIS Cloud Management System is an initiative from SaSa's IT team that works with third-party software providers, marketplaces, exchanges, and consortiums to integrate Procurement, Ordering, Expense, and Time and Labor management solutions. It enables companies to optimize their ordering, expensing, and purchasing processes to better manage their business needs. Its cloud solutions are flexible and promote easy ERP integration and third-party system integration. The SIS suite of applications helps you save money on all of your business expenses, combined with the lower overall cost of cloud computing, for a truly money-saving solution. It's the perfect underpinning for your procurement, online ordering, and expense management strategy. Below are the modules.

SIS Online Ordering System is focused on the user's order experience from beginning to end. Armed with a sleek look and feel, the simplified navigation lets users browse visually at every level of the hierarchy. The customizable shopping list format helps customers become more efficient in their ordering, so they can sort by category and part number for frequently ordered items. Our solution helps you seamlessly set up a digital storefront with an impeccable shopping experience, enabling you to sell products directly to your end customers in just a few clicks — better value for your clients, and better profits for you.

SIS Expense Management System is a travel and expense solution that streamlines and automates travel spend management. It enhances value by integrating seamlessly with Oracle EBS Internet Expense and SAP's Travel Expense Management System, driving better decision-making, sustainable financial discipline, regulatory compliance, and optimized business processes — all at the lowest cost.

SIS Procurement Management System is a powerful procure-to-pay system that brings consumer e-commerce shopping ease and bottleneck-busting functionality to the procurement workflow.

SIS Time and Labor Management System automates the entire time and attendance record-keeping process, providing an intuitive, web-based, and mobile (iPhone, iPad, Android, and Windows phone) interface for time entry and approval.
